How Maxio Metering Works

Capture Usage Events in Real-Time

Maxio Metering ingests usage data directly from your products or services. Capture events instantly, ensuring that nothing slips through the cracks—no delays, no missed revenue, and no guesswork.

Aggregate and Apply Pricing Automatically

Define your pricing once, and let Maxio do the rest. Aggregate data based on geographic location, user segments, or custom attributes, and apply your pricing rules automatically, eliminating manual processes and billing inaccuracies.

Generate Immediate, Accurate Invoices

Turn aggregated usage data into precise, detailed invoices right away. Improve your cash flow by ensuring timely, accurate billing every single time—building trust and transparency with your customers.
